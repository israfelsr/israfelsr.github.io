Hi, I’m Israfel Salazar!

I’m a last semester MSc. student at the Unversité Paris-Saclay on the Master Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence. I’m a research intern at the Statify team at Inria.

My interests lie in visual and language understanding with focus on human-machine interaction. I’m also interested in motion learning and visual-language navigation. In other topics I also like reading about animal cognition and ancient history.

I started my career as a mechanical engineer studying theoretical and applied physics at the University of Chile. I worked for one year and a half as a robotics engineer at Mundos Virtuales. I also collaborated with the Coastal NLP Group working on multitask learning for optical character recognition.

In this blog I’ll be documenting some learning notes, papers summaries and others. You can find my resume here.
